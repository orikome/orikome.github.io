Night Train VR (2022)

Night Train is a linear horror-puzzle VR game experienced in the cabin of a train. It was part of a school course and was finished with a group of 7 people. We managed to create around 20 minutes of gameplay, which follows a storyline filled with puzzles and horror elements. I made an endlessly generating scenery for the game, programmed a few individual gameplay mechanics, and created a few 3D-models.

Endlessly generating scenery

The way the endless scenery works is by giving an illusion of a moving environment, meaning the train itself doesn't actually move. Tiles filled with randomly positioned trees and rocks are spawned in and they start moving forward, which makes it seem like the train is moving. These tiles are then despawned as they move further away from the player and new tiles are spawned in.


Below is a function that is used by the endless generator to spawn in tiles. 

public void SpawnTile(int amount)
    {
        for (int i = 0; i < amount; i++)
        {
            if(curTilesAmount < maxTileAmount) //tiles are only spawned in if there is space
            {
                    GameObject clone = Instantiate(forestTiles[Random.Range(0, forestTiles.Length)],
                    				   new Vector3(nextSpawnPoint.position.x, nextSpawnPoint.position.y,
						   nextSpawnPoint.position.z - errorMargin),
                    				   Quaternion.identity);
                    curTiles.Add(clone);  //add the tile object to a list
                    tilesSpawnedAmount++;
                    clone.name = "Block " + tilesSpawnedAmount;
                    GetNextSpawnPoint(); //next spawn point is pulled from the block spawned before
            }
        }
    }

Storyline

The game begins inside a train cabin and you hear an announcement related to your trip. After this, you are greeted by the train conductor who asks you for your student card. After a while, a grandma next door starts up a conversation with you, and suggests you listen to the radio. After turning on the radio and hearing something odd coming from one of the channels, unusual things start to happen...

Tahti VR (2021-2022)

Tahti VR is a fast-paced VR rhythm game where you shoot targets and dodge incoming obstacles to the beat of the music. The game began as a part of a school project, developed by 4 people in total. We have signed a contract with Kajak Games to release the game in late 2022 on Steam. My main role has been to create audiovisual elements to the game, which include having the environment react to the music in different ways. One of which is the hexagon rings you see at the background of the screenshot, and also particles which use a flocking algorithm to move around. I also worked on the overall visuals such as the background colors, weapon lasers and target explosions.

Gameplay Loop

The goal of the game is to collect points by shooting targets that appear to the rhythm of the music, while also dodging incoming obstacles that come your way. Shooting a target accumulates points in your combo meter, which increases the player's points by 2x, 4x or with an 8x multiplier respectively. Missing a target or colliding with an obstacle breaks your combo and lowers the combo meter, and the game is lost if the meter goes low enough. The playable maps have predetermined spots for the targets to appear, which are placed inside a MIDI-file.

Future Plans

We are currently in the process of setting up a Steam page for the game and creating enough content for the game to be ready for release.
